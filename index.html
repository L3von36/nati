WHAT WE DO

Our Services

We provide services across the highway and transport project lifecycle, delivered as standalone work packages or sub-consultancy arrangements.

Highway Engineering & Road Design

  • Highway geometric design (rural and urban roads)
  • Pavement design (flexible and rigid)
  • Intersection/interchange concept support
  • Quantity take-off (QTO) and BoQ support
  • Tender drawings, specifications, and IFC documentation
  • As-built drawings and design updates

Traffic Engineering & Road Safety

  • Traffic counts and analysis support
  • Traffic impact assessments
  • Road safety audits (design-stage & existing roads)
  • Signing and marking design support
  • Safety improvement prioritization

Geotechnical Engineering

  • Soil investigation data interpretation
  • Subgrade evaluation and classification
  • Bearing capacity and settlement analysis
  • Slope stability analysis
  • Geotechnical reporting
  • Earthworks analysis

Hydraulics & Drainage

  • Highway drainage design
  • Culvert hydraulic analysis
  • Catchment and hydrology analysis
  • Flood risk screening (pre-feasibility)

Construction Supervision & Technical Support

  • Construction methodology review
  • Site engineering support
  • Variation order and measurement support
  • Progress documentation

Feasibility & Project Management Support

  • Feasibility studies (technical options, constraints, quantities)
  • Tender preparation and bid evaluation
  • PMO-style reporting (schedule, cost, risk)
  • Transport and logistics planning
